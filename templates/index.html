About this site

Hi there {{ session.screen_name.name }}!

This site is made for the pure purpose of being a portfolio project (as well as being the final project for one of the programming courses I took). The full code is available at GitHub, so please feel free to fork to your hearts content and use this as a base for creating improved version of this application! :)

This smal website let's you check out any (non-protected) user on Twitter giving you publicly available details on them (like their timezone, location, number of tweets, followers, people being followed by them, followers to following ratio, etc.), and shows you their activity times. It also presents you with your most recent tweets, mentions and Direct Messages received), let's you post a tweet directly from the website (without having to access Twitter separately) and shows you your 30 newest followers.

I hope you'll enjoy the experience!
